About the Villa

Where the Ionian Sea Meets Absolute Serenity

Nestled among olive groves overlooking the shimmering waters of the Ionian Sea, The Oasis is a newly built luxury villa that redefines island living. Every detail has been crafted with intention — from the infinity pool that merges with the horizon to the hand-selected marble and natural stone that ground the home in its Grecian setting.

Just minutes from the vibrant village of Nidri and the legendary beaches of Lefkada, this is your private sanctuary.
